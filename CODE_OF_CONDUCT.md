# ShitSoft Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

At **ShitSoft**, we believe great software is built by people who are willing to learn, experiment, and share knowledge.

We are committed to creating an open, respectful, and collaborative environment where developers of all experience levels can contribute, ask questions, make mistakes, and improve together.

Everyone who participates in our community is expected to act with respect and help create a place where curiosity and creativity can thrive.

## Our Standards

Examples of positive behavior include:

* Being respectful and constructive in discussions.
* Helping others learn and improve.
* Sharing knowledge and giving useful feedback.
* Accepting different approaches and opinions.
* Admitting mistakes and learning from them.
* Focusing on improving projects and the community.

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

* Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
* Deliberately insulting or humiliating others.
* Trolling or disrupting discussions.
* Sharing private information without permission.
* Malicious behavior that harms the community or its projects.
* Any behavior that creates a hostile environment for contributors.

## Technical Discussions

We encourage strong opinions and technical debates.

Disagreeing with an idea, design decision, or implementation is completely acceptable.

However:

* Criticize the idea, not the person.
* Explain your reasoning.
* Be open to changing your mind.
* Remember that every contributor is here to build something better.

A good technical argument makes a project stronger.

## Enforcement

Community leaders are responsible for maintaining these standards.

Actions may include:

1. **Correction**

   Minor issues may receive a private reminder or clarification.

2. **Warning**

   Repeated or inappropriate behavior may result in an official warning.

3. **Temporary Restrictions**

   Serious violations may result in temporary removal from community spaces.

4. **Permanent Removal**

   Continued harassment, malicious behavior, or severe violations may result in permanent removal.

Moderation decisions will be made fairly and with the goal of protecting the community.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all ShitSoft spaces, including:

* GitHub repositories
* Discussions and issues
* Pull requests
* Official communication channels
* Community events

It also applies when someone represents ShitSoft publicly.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please contact the community maintainers.

Reports will be reviewed respectfully and handled as privately as possible.

## Final Note

ShitSoft is built around curiosity, experimentation, and collaboration.

Everyone starts somewhere. Everyone makes mistakes.

The goal is not to create a perfect community — it is to create one where people can learn, build, and improve together.
